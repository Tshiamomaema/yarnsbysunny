Our Story

Discover the passion behind Yarns by Sunny

Meet Sunny

Founder & Creative Director

Hello! I'm Sunny, the heart and soul behind Yarns. What began as a passion for creating beautiful, sustainable fashion has blossomed into a brand that celebrates individuality and conscious living.

With over a decade of experience in the fashion industry, I've made it my mission to create pieces that are not only stylish but also kind to our planet. Every garment is thoughtfully designed and ethically produced.

When I'm not designing new collections, you can find me exploring local markets for unique textiles or sharing styling tips with our wonderful community.

Our Philosophy

At Yarns, we believe in fashion that tells a story. Each piece is crafted with intention, blending contemporary design with timeless elegance.

We're committed to sustainability, using eco-friendly materials and ethical production methods to minimize our environmental impact while creating beautiful, long-lasting garments.
